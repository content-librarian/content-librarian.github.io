ISBN parser

Extract publisher registration numbers from ISBNs.


Getting ready . . .

Upload a spreadsheet with columns named "ISBN," "PARENT," "PUBLISHER," and "IMPRINT."

You'll need at least ISBNs in the ISBN column and something to indicate a publisher in one of the other columns, but if you don't have all the columns, that's okay.

If your spreadsheet is in XLSX format, your columns need to be in the order above.

If your spreadsheet is in CSV format, your columns can be in any order, but they need to be named as above (all caps, too).
